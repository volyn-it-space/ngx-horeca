Housekeeper

A hotel cleanliness role focused on room preparation, guest comfort, laundry coordination, and public area care.

The housekeeper plays a key role in hotel comfort. Clean rooms, fresh linen, and prepared guest spaces are essential for a good stay.

This role requires accuracy, responsibility, and attention to details that guests immediately notice.

Housekeeping works closely with reception to keep room availability clear and guest arrivals smooth.

Responsibilities

  • Clean and prepare hotel rooms for guest arrival
  • Change bed linen, towels, and room supplies
  • Report maintenance issues or missing items
  • Keep public hotel areas clean and tidy
  • Coordinate with reception about room readiness

Requirements

  • Attention to cleanliness and details
  • Responsible attitude toward guest property
  • Ability to follow room preparation standards
  • Readiness for active physical work

Benefits

  • Stable daytime work
  • Training on hotel standards
  • Team support and clear checklists
  • Growth into senior housekeeper or housekeeping supervisor
